Item 5.

Now let us take some 5-dimensional examples in the book Adjoint and Trivial Cohomology Tables for Indecomposable Nilpotent Lie Algebras of Dimension \(\leq 7\) over \(\mathbb{C}\) by Magnin, namely

\begin{equation*} \mathcal{G}_{5,6}=\mathrm{scNLA}[5, 6],\;\mathcal{G}_{5,4}\dagger=\mathrm{scNLA}[5, 9],\;\mathcal{G}_{5,5}\dagger=\mathrm{scNLA}[5, 7],\;\text{and}\;\mathcal{G}_{5,2}\dagger=\mathrm{scNLA}[5, 8]. \end{equation*}

For each of these algebras, we will print out the list of all dimensions of cocycle spaces

\begin{equation*} \mathrm{dim}\,Z^p(\mathfrak{g},\mathbb{C})\quad\text{for}\quad 0\leq p\leq\mathrm{dim}\,5 \end{equation*}

as well as the list of cohomology dimensions

\begin{equation*} \mathrm{dim}\,H^p(\mathfrak{g},\mathbb{C})\quad\text{for}\quad 0\leq p\leq\mathrm{dim}\,5 \end{equation*}

in order to compare them with those furnished by Magin.

This matches the lists given by Magnin.
