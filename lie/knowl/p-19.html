Then the trivial cohomology modules are defined by

\begin{equation*} H^m(\mathfrak{g},K):=\mathrm{Ker}(d^{m+1})/\mathrm{Im}(d^m) \end{equation*}

so that the Betti numbers of \(\mathfrak{g}\) are given by

\begin{align*} b_m:&=\mathrm{dim}\left(H^m(\mathfrak{g},K)\right)\\ &=\mathrm{dim}\left(\mathrm{Ker}(d^{m+1})\right)-\mathrm{dim}\left(\mathrm{Im}(d^m)\right)\\ &=\binom{n}{m}-\mathrm{rank}\left(d^{m+1}\right)-\mathrm{rank}\left(d^m\right). \end{align*}
