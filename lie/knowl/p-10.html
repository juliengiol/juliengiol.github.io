Let us consider the parameterized family of 6-dimensional Lie algebras \(L_{6,19}(t)\) with nonzero brackets

\begin{equation*} [x_1,x_2]=x_4,\quad [x_1,x_3]=x_5,\quad [x_2,x_4]=x_6,\quad [x_3,x_5]=tx_6 \end{equation*}

and store it in a dictionary containing the base field, the dimension, the table, and the structure constants. Notice that we choose to see it as a 6-dimensional algebra over the rational function field \(\mathbb{Q}(t)\text{.}\)

Now here is how we can set the variable \(t\) to be equal to, say, \(t_0=e^{2i\pi/3}\text{,}\) while working with the cyclotomic field \(\mathbb{Q}\left(e^{2i\pi/3}\right)\) generated by the latter and printing out the structure constants \((c_{ij}^6)\text{.}\)
