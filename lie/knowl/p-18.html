In trivial cohomology, the coboundary operator

\begin{equation*} d^m:\Lambda^{m-1}\mathfrak{g}^*\longrightarrow\Lambda^m\mathfrak{g}^* \end{equation*}

is defined by

\begin{equation*} d^mf(x_1,\ldots,x_m):=\sum_{1\le i<j\le m}(-1)^{i+j}f\left([x_i,x_j],\ldots,\widehat{x_i},\ldots,\widehat{x_j},\ldots,x_m\right). \end{equation*}
