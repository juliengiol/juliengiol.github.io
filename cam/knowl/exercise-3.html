  5. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\frac{\sin x}{\tan x}\)

  7. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x\rightarrow 0}\frac{\sqrt{1+2x}-\sqrt{1-4x}}{x}\)
